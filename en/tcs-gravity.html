About us

About Gravity

TCS Gravity is the competitive section of the Mountain Trail Builders Association – TCS, dedicated to the development of competitive mountain biking in the downhill and enduro disciplines. The section was founded on the legacy of two clubs – ŠKTD Avče and Pedal Cerkno – which concluded their sporting journey after many years of activity. Their heritage, knowledge and racing experience live on today in a new generation of riders under the TCS Gravity banner.

Our riders

Team

Our riders come from across the Soča Valley and the wider region, united by their passion for mountain biking, racing and developing the sport locally. They compete in the SloEnduro series and the national downhill cup, both held under the umbrella of the Cycling Federation of Slovenia. The section operates within the TCS club, which has been bringing together mountain biking enthusiasts and trail builders for over 15 years. Together we drive the growth of the cycling community and the building and maintenance of mountain bike trails that enable quality training and rider progression.

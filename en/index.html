About us

The story of TCS NG began more than twenty years ago, although we formally established ourselves as an association in 2019. Our mission is to preserve, build, and develop high-quality trails in cooperation with nature and the local environment.

We operate primarily in the Goriška region and its surroundings, where we clean and maintain existing trails which, thanks to their natural features, scenic views, and thoughtful design, rank among the most beautiful in Slovenia. In total, we maintain around 40 kilometers of trails suitable for running, hiking, and mountain biking.

We see mountain biking as an activity that strengthens the body, encourages social interaction, and connects people with nature. We believe that properly designed and maintained trails allow safe use with minimal environmental impact while also contributing to the development of recreation and tourism in the wider region. Cooperation with like-minded organizations and relevant institutions is essential to achieving this.

About us

The story of TCS ng started more than twenty years ago, and we formally founded the club in 2019. Our mission is to maintain, build and develop high-quality mountain bike trails in cooperation with nature and the local environment.

We work in the areas of Skalnica, Sabotin, Škabrijel, Mark and beyond—clearing neglected paths and building singletracks that, thanks to terrain, views and thoughtful design, rank among the best in Slovenia.
